About Oliver Lane's


Oliver Lane is a restaurant based in Kemanggisan, Jakarta. Serving since 2020, we have satisfied over 10,000 customers. Our goal and passion is to give you the best dining experience you will ever have and we hope that you will come again.


Our dishes varies from vegans to cuisines for meat lovers. Ingredients are hand-picked and are ensured to be fresh and have the best quality possible. Through this process of selection, we are able to make the food you love.
